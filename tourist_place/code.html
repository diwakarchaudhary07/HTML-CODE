Top 10 Tourist Places in India

1. Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra and is a symbol of love.

2. Jaipur

Jaipur is known as the Pink City and is famous for palaces.

3. Kerala

Kerala is famous for its backwaters and greenery.

4. Goa

Goa is popular for beaches and nightlife.

5. Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station with snow mountains.

6. Ladakh

Ladakh is known for adventure and mountain views.

7. Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the oldest cities in the world.

8. Delhi

Delhi is the capital of India with many monuments.

9. Darjeeling

Darjeeling is famous for tea gardens and cool weather.

10. Andaman Islands

Andaman Islands are known for clean beaches and blue water.
